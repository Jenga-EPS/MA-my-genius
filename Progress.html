My Progress in Rocket league

I got Rocket league in the summer of 2019, but began playing it often in the fall of that year. I grew up playing sports and playing car video games, and Rocket League seemed to be the perfect mix between the 2. I began playing on my xbox for a long time, but never made it through Platinum. I was stuck in platinum for 8 months, but finally, something changed.

In 2020, Epic Games buys out Psyonix. Epic games then makes Rocket League a free to play game on all platforms. When Rocket League came out as free to play, As soon as I switched, I made it out of platinum, through diamond, and all the way to Champion without losing a single game. I then was stuck in the Champion rank for 14 months until I finally broke through to Grand Champion 1. 2 seasons ago I broke through to Grand Champion 2 but have since dropped back down to low Grand Champion 1.
