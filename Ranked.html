Rocket League Rank System

Just like every competitive game, Rocket League has a rank tree. Each rank has 3 tiers, and each tier has 4 divisions. Ranks are tracked using something called mmr (Match Making Rating) and you can gain mmr by winning games. Each rank has its own name and color.

  • Bronze: The lowest rank, clueless about the game. Associated with bronze or brown
  • Silver: The second lowest rank, still sort of clueless. Associated with silver or grey
  • Gold: Beginning to understand how to play the game. Associated with Gold or yellow
  • Platinum: The most toxic rank, you think you are good but you really aren't. Large growth period. Associated with teal
  • Diamond: Beginning to get a hold on mechanics such as air dribbles and ceiling shots. Associated with blue
  • Champion: Starting to get good, you can beat most of the playerbase. Associated with purple
  • Grand Champion: This was the old highest rank and used to be every player's dream. You are in the top 1% of players. Associated with red
  • Supersonic Legend: You are playing against pros and are the best of the best. Associated with White or purple
